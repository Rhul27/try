Our Team

Meet Our Engineer

Welders

Welders are skilled workers who join or fuse metal parts together using various welding techniques, such as arc welding, MIG welding, or TIG welding. They play a crucial role in the fabrication and assembly of crane components.

Turner

Turners, also known as lathe operators, operate lathe machines to shape and cut metal parts with precision. They work with rotating metal pieces and use cutting tools to create cylindrical or conical shapes required for crane components.

Electricians

Electricians specialize in electrical systems and play a vital role in the installation, wiring, and maintenance of electrical components in cranes. They ensure that the electrical systems are functioning properly and meet safety standards.

Painters

Painters are involved in the finishing process of the crane components. They apply paint or protective coatings to the metal surfaces to enhance their appearance and protect against corrosion.
